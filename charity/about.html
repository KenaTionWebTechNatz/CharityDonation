INSIPIRATION TO IDONATE FORMATION

A little inspiration to what brought us to this idea,that of coming up with iDONATE, Malnutrition has been a very grave problem in our country as it mainly affects the northern region counties. These places are hard to access and as a result many innocent lives end up being lost due lack of adequate food supplies and very harsh conditions that forces many to not be able to make ends meet.And with that we sought out to come up with a web application that will not only create awareness of existing areas but also be able to make donations to those affected areas that can be in form of food items or money.

iDONATE MISSION

Our mission is to be able to first create awareness on areas that are worst hit by malnutrition.After creating awareness we then create a platform that will enable people to be able to donate and the donations can be in form of either food stuff or means of payment .We will ensure that through these donations, they will be delivered to the affected areas as soon as possible.
